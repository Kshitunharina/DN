Our Journey

In 2019,our founder nephew is suffering from the deadly disease Thalassemia, which the patient has to infuse with blood every other day.
For the same, they brought the patient to Jaipur where the doctor asked for 40 Lac INR,they completed the amount but unfortunately, it becomes unsuccessful.

Then everyone starts saying to bring the patient to Chennai, but after paying such a huge amount of 40 lac INR it's hard for a middle-class family to again fill the same amount which has been asked for plus the allowances. So they decided to take him back to kota! After a few days, his parents got a call that their fees have been filled by an NGO, now we can treat your child!! And unfortunately, it's too late and we lost him.

But at the cemetery when our founder Mr.WASEEM AHMED talked to his father they smiled and said an NGO filled the fees and had a soothing smile on his face that in today world such pure souls exist.

From that day Mr.WASEEM decide to open an ngo, by which we can help needy! But initially we could not help with money so we chose education and started free classes.

So along with Mr. AADIL and mr. SUHAIL we start working on ground level and start connecting people to us.
